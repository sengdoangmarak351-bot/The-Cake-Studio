About Us

Oishi brings the finest Japanese and Korean cuisine to Tura. Our menu features authentic dishes made from fresh ingredients, from sizzling yakitori and crispy tempura to hearty ramen and flavorful bibimbap. Whether you're a sushi lover or exploring Asian flavors for the first time, we promise a delightful experience.

We are open Monday through Saturday, from 10:30 AM to 8:30 PM (kitchen closes at 8:30 PM). Reservations are recommended to ensure you get a table at your preferred time.
